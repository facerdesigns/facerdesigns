Practice Areas

What We Handle

Legal disciplines.
Delivered with precision.

  • 01Corporate, Commercial & Trade Law+
    Company formation, shareholder agreements, mergers & acquisitions, corporate restructuring, trade and investment advisory, and ongoing commercial counsel for businesses operating in Zimbabwe and across the region.
  • 02Litigation & Dispute Resolution+
    Civil and commercial litigation in the High Court and Supreme Court of Zimbabwe, arbitration proceedings, mediation, and debt recovery.
  • 03Criminal Law Practice+
    Defence representation across the full spectrum of criminal matters, from bail applications and plea proceedings to full trials in the Magistrates Court and High Court.
  • 04Employment & Labour Law+
    Employment contracts, disciplinary hearings, Labour Court proceedings, retrenchment advisory, and workplace policy compliance under the Labour Act.
  • 05Property, Conveyancing & Notarial Practice+
    Residential and commercial property transfers, lease agreements, title deed investigations, mortgage bonds, notarial deeds, and sectional title matters.
  • 06Intellectual Property, Patents & Trademarks+
    Trademark registration and agency work with ZIPO, patent applications, copyright advisory, IP licensing, blockchain and technology IP, and enforcement against infringement.
  • 07Environmental Law+
    Environmental impact assessments, regulatory compliance under the Environmental Management Act, disputes with ZINWA and EMA, and environmental due diligence for transactions.
  • 08Entertainment & Media Law+
    Contracts for artists, producers, broadcasters, and promoters; licensing and royalty agreements; rights protection; and disputes in the creative and media industries.
  • 09Banking & Finance+
    Loan documentation, security agreements, financial regulatory compliance under RBZ guidelines, and structured finance transactions.
  • 10Tax & Revenue+
    Tax planning, ZIMRA dispute resolution, formal objections and appeals before the Fiscal Appeal Court, and transactional tax advisory.
  • 11Constitutional & Administrative Law+
    Constitutional rights challenges, judicial review applications against administrative decisions, and proceedings before the Constitutional Court.
  • 12Family, Estates & Succession+
    Divorce and dissolution, child custody and maintenance, estate planning, will drafting, deceased estate administration, and inheritance disputes.
  • 13Blockchain & Technology Law+
    Legal advisory on blockchain technology, digital assets, cryptocurrency transactions, smart contracts, and the regulatory landscape for technology businesses in Zimbabwe.
